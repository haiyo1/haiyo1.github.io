I felt somewhat inspired by the recent blog post. :> If anyone was reading weebs chat circa summer 2019 then you might have seen some ideas I had floating around for a "CM ToB" and they were total bs. Instakills every couple of ticks, freeze immunity and other things you'd probably hate to play. I challenged myself to do better this time.
Mistress of Sugadinti Levels: 5,000 (~1.43x normal) 350 350 200 450 (~1.29x normal) 350 Aggressive stats: +0 +0 +300 +0 +0 +0 Defensive stats: +0 +0 +0 +0 +0 Unless stated otherwise, the stats are identical to normal Maiden's. Immune to poison Immune to venom
Blood spawn Levels: 200 (~1.67x normal) 1 1 0 0 1 Aggressive stats: +0 +0 +0 +0 +0 +0 Defensive stats: +0 +0 +0 +0 +0 Unless stated otherwise, the stats are identical to the normal blood spawns. Immune to poison Immune to venom
The scaling for the Mistress' max hit and blood damage is slightly different from Maiden's. Maiden Auto Attacks - 36.5 + 3.5 * number of nylocas leaked Maiden Blood Damage - Minimum damage of 5 - Maximum damage of 10 + 2 * number of nylocas leaked Mistress Auto Attacks - 47 + 5 * number of nylocas leaked Mistress Blood Damage - Minimum damage of 7 - Maximum damage of 8 + 3 * number of nylocas leaked
The Mistress' basic attack is similar to that of the Maiden's, only that she attacks with a blood tornado. It shares many similarities to her predecessor's attack: - 10 tick speed; - Ignores all magic defence; - Max hit is increased when Nylocas are able to reach her; - Damage reduced in half with Protect from Magic. Blood tornadoes do not reduce stats. Instead, they will inflict the targeted player with a blood trail. A blood trail is functionally identical to Olm's acid trail attack. The damage of the blood puddles left behind is identical to those from maiden's blood attacks - and thus, can also be increased when Nylocas reach her. It will also drain prayer if stepped on, and the Mistress will heal for the damage it deals. The trail lasts for 10 ticks, meaning if the same player is repeatedly targeted, they will constantly have a blood trail. Similarly, the blood tiles generated from a blood trail will also last for 10 ticks each. Blood tornadoes that target the same player multiple times in a row will deal 10% reduced damage each. This stacks up to 3 times at a total of 30% reduced damage if the player has tanked 4 or more tornadoes in a row. Note that this reduction stacks multiplicatively with prayer reduction. Since the Mistress' magic level is roughly 1.29x Maiden's, a player who is consistently tanking can expect to take the same amount of damage as they would in Maiden.
Blood spawns do not spontaneously spawn during this fight. There is only a chance of a blood spawn appearing when a player walks on a blood puddle. However, every 3 basic attacks (does not include the blood toss attack), the Mistress will spawn 2 blood spawns in two of the locations where Nylocas can spawn (SW tile). In this state, blood spawns behave identically to the ones found in Maiden's fight.
Compared to Maiden's blood toss attack, the Mistress has a larger spread. Two additional nearby blood puddles are cast at every player, rather than only one. Similarly to Maiden, the Mistress heals from damage dealt by this attack. Whenever the Mistress casts this attack, she calls in all living blood spawns to her. They will stop randomly traversing the tiles of the room and attempt to path towards her. If any spawn succeeds in making it to her, she will become invulnerable for 8 ticks and cast the Blood Tide special attack. All remaining blood spawns return to their usual wandering behavior if this happens.
Vadnylocas are stronger variants of the Nylocas found in the normal Theatre of Blood. Vadnylocas Matomenos exist in three different forms. Their behavior in this fight is identical to that of the Nylocas Matomenos, but they can only be killed using a specific style, correspondent to their Ischyros, Toxobolos and Hagios counterparts. Furthermore, if a player attacks one with the incorrect style, it will be completely immune to damage from that player.
The three variants of Vadnylocas Matomenos. Can only be killed using melee, ranged and magic respectively. Similar to Maiden, up to 10 Vadnylocas Matomenos will spawn when the Mistress reaches 70, 50 and 30% of her total HP. They will attempt to walk into the Mistress. In these spawns, a random assortment of melee, range and mage variants will be generated, with at least one of each style being guaranteed. In addition to healing the Mistress and buffing her attacks, if a Vadnylocas is able to reach her, she will become invulnerable for 8 ticks and cast the Blood Tide special attack. Note that remaining Vadnylocas will continue to path towards her and provide both buffs and heals if successful - however, only one Vadnylocas from each set (70/50/30%) can trigger the Blood Tide. Trivia: Vad means "wild" in Hungarian.
At 15% of her HP, the Mistress will cast the Blood Tide special attack. However, she will not go invulnerable (this only occurs if a blood spawn or Vadnylocas reaches her). The Blood Tide forms a huge pool of blood behind the Mistress, which periodically deals 2-6 damage every tick to any player standing on it for 3 ticks. After those 3 ticks, the blood darkens and the damage value changes to that of the blood puddles (therefore dealing far more damage to anyone within the pool). Shortly after, waves of blood slide across the floor all the way through the arena and to the entrance. The damage dealt by these waves is an increased version of the blood damage scaling. Blood Wave Damage: - Minimum damage of 14 - Maximum damage of 22 + 3 * number of nylocas leaked Being hit with a blood wave has a chance of triggering a blood spawn. Furthermore, the Mistress will heal from damage dealt with this attack. The waves are fired in predictable alternating patterns of three or two rows with five or six waves each. The middle wave of each row will travel at 3 tiles per tick, the adjacent ones at 2 tiles per tick, and the outer ones at 1 tile per tick. They fire every 5 ticks, a total of 5 times. The blood pool is formed over the course of 3 ticks, and remains for 5 ticks after the last wave, meaning the full special attack takes 28 ticks (16.8 seconds). Note that the waves cannot be "ran through" - similarly to Sepulchre arrows. During this time, the Mistress does not use other attacks at all.
