The chance of unique loot is rolled individually per player with every completion. The only requirement to land a roll on the table is to deal at least 2% of the Colossus' True HP (which includes the Cores and the Phase 16 heal) in damage. Unique drop table
The sword of the colossus is a two-handed slash weapon requiring 85 Attack and Strength to wield, available as a rare drop from the Marble Colossus. Each time the player attacks with this weapon, they will be drained for 8 run energy. In addition, every attack will render the player unable to move for 3 ticks. This absurdly large weapon is one of the heaviest items in the game (30 kg), and significantly reduces player mobility when attacking with it. However, if used properly, its damage output is rivaled only by the Scythe of vitur against 3x3 NPCs with low Defence levels, and outclasses any weapon against higher Defence targets.
The marble seal is the strongest defensive neckwear slot item, and requires 85 Defence to wear. It is only obtainable as a rare drop from the Marble Colossus. On top of boasting the highest defence bonuses against all attack styles, the seal has a Discharge option which unveils its true defensive potential. When activated, all players within a 7x7 area around the caster (including the caster's square) will have their Defence level boosted by 10 + 20% of the player's base level (rounded down). This is the highest possible Defence level increase, reaching a boosted level of 128 at level 99. In addition to this, they will also be granted 20% damage reduction. Note that the damage reduction does NOT stack with other damage reducing items, such as the Dinh's bulwark and Justiciar armour. The boost will last for 30 seconds upon activation. The Defence level boost does not naturally decrease during the duration of the effect, but can be reduced by enemy spells or potions like the Zamorak brew (similarly to divine potions). It can be activated an infinite number of times, but only once every 5 minutes, regardless of how many marble seals are owned. Similarly, players who receive the seal's power from another player cannot be boosted again for 5 minutes.
Attempting to use the Discharge option while it is on cooldown will result in the chatbox message: "The seal appears dormant. Judging by its coloration, it will be pulsing with energy in around X minutes. Players do not have to wear the seal in order to use its Discharge option. However, players who do not have the 85 Defence requirement to wield it cannot use it at all. Attempting to activate it will result in the chatbox message: "Your resolve is too faint to summon the seal's power." Players cannot bring the marble seal into the Duel Arena to gain an advantage if the "no potions" option is enabled.
The magical lodestone is a ring that requires Level 85 Magic to wield and can only be received as a rare drop from the Marble Colossus. The ring has a passive effect that reduces the player's run energy drain rate by 15% while in combat. This ring grants purely offensive bonuses, surpassing the Brimstone ring as an effective melee and ranged ring - though it falls short in magic accuracy. It is also marginally worse than the Archers ring (i) for ranging. Most notably, it can in some cases be considered superior to the Berserker (i). If both rings result in the same melee max hit with a specific gear setup, the Magical lodestone has an edge in melee accuracy increase. It also has a passive effect identical to that of the Ring of endurance's secondary effect, though only active during combat.
The breastplate of new life is part of the New life armour set, requiring Level 85 Hitpoints and Defence to wield. It is a rare drop from the Marble Colossus. This piece of armour has a passive effect which overheals 14 Hitpoints when its user is at full Hitpoints, and is out of combat. Note that this effect does NOT stack with other overhealing items, such as Anglerfish and Saradomin brews, with the exception of other New life armour pieces.
The cuisse of new life is part of the New life armour set, requiring Level 85 Hitpoints and Defence to wield. It is a rare drop from the Marble Colossus. This piece of leg armour has a passive effect which overheals 12 Hitpoints when its user is at full Hitpoints, and is out of combat. Note that this effect does NOT stack with other overhealing items, such as Anglerfish and Saradomin brews, with the exception of other New life armour pieces.
The barbute of new life is part of the New life armour set, requiring Level 85 Hitpoints and Defence to wield. It is a rare drop from the Marble Colossus. This helmet has a passive effect which overheals 10 Hitpoints when its user is at full Hitpoints, and is out of combat. Note that this effect does NOT stack with other overhealing items, such as Anglerfish and Saradomin brews, with the exception of other New life armour pieces. Each piece of the armor was once a part of the Colossus' body, retaining some of his strength. When combined, however, the very essence of his being can be felt living within.
When the full New life armour set is equipped, on top of a grand total of an additional 36 Hitpoints, the player gains the Reawakened set effect: 30% of damage taken is rebound to their opponent (rounded down). The armour set can occasionally serve as a good defensive alternative to other melee armour. Due its lacklustre defensive bonuses, it is especially strong when paired with a Marble seal, combining excellent Defence and Hitpoints boosts. It also provides a slight boost to Magic defence, which is unique to all melee armour with the exception of Void Knight equipment. However, its Hitpoints boost can also be used as an offensive option when wielding the full set. Due to the set effect, players may opt to have a larger Hitpoints pool to intentionally take more damage, which results in more damage against their opponent.
Marble midget is a pet obtained from the Marble Colossus.
Players can use Metamorphosis option to change the Colossus to its void-based variant.
