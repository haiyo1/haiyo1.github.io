What? This is an OSRS adaptation of a RotMG boss (Marble Colossus) that I made as a fun little game design exercise. It is intended as one of the final bosses for a theoretical Raids X, which can fit up to 75 players, with the difficulty level aiming to be even higher than ToB. Though I personally take issue with the amount of one-hit K.O.s in ToB, it has a lot of interesting mechanics which I tried to explore further. You'll also find inspiration from CoX mechanics, sprinkled in with a bit of Inferno, Nightmare and Sepulchre. And of course, I tried to keep the overall fight very similar to the original game - just with a completely new gameplay system. I also tried to make sure that everything here is within the game's programming limits. Nothing in here should require engine work, but of course that's just speculation. The arena is a perfect 33x33 square, with the Marble Colossus spawning in the center. Standing underneath the Marble Colossus at any point results in rapid trample damage (10-15 per tick). On the 9th tile away from each corner there is a Marble Pillar, 4 in total. It is a 1x1 stationary NPC. It cannot be used to protect against any of the colossus' attacks. The colossus does not get stuck on the pillars either. The entrance to the room seals off immediately as the fight begins. Players cannot enter or leave mid fight. Players who die will be unable to continue participating.
"Look upon my mighty bulwark." The Colossus activates the Marble Pillars. From this point onward, they will be continually respawned throughout the fight every 90 seconds (150 ticks). Any pillars that are still surviving when this timer expires will self destruct. These will continuously fire at all players within a 17x17 area around their central tile. This means that all players will be targeted by at least one pillar. Players standing in the central rows of the room will be targeted by two pillars. Anyone standing in the central tile of the room will be targeted by all 4 pillars.
Each pillar covers a quadrant of the room. Blue lines are covered by two pillars. If the pillar's defence is reduced to 0, it will be deactivated for 30 seconds (50 ticks). During this time, it is unable to attack. This can only be done once per pillar. Pillars can deal up to 22 damage, but Protect from Magic reduces it by 50%, down to 11. They attack every 4 ticks. However, they will not attack until 6 ticks after spawning. In addition, pillars will spawn with certain colors etched onto the marble, casting different types of spells accordingly. The colors with which the pillars spawn is random. Each time a pillar respawns, it will always have a different color to its predecessor. Purple: Pillar attacks ignore defence and all forms of damage reduction (including protection prayers). White: Reduces Attack and Strength levels by 20% (only applies once, though will be reapplied if stats are restored). Orange: Reduces Ranged and Magic levels by 20% (only applies once, though will be reapplied if stats are restored). Red: Reduces Defence level by 20% (only applies once, though will be reapplied if stats are restored), and renders all healing less effective by 20% for 10 seconds. Green: Each attack reduces 5 run energy. Blue: Each attack reduces 5 prayer. Pink: Inflicts the player with recurrent damage for 10 seconds - all damage taken will result in additional damage equivalent to 20% of the original hitsplat. (edited)
Note that these effects cannot be protected against. They will inflict players regardless of prayer, damage or accuracy roll.
